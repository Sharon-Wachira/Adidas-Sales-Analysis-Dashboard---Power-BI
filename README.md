# Adidas-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard - Power-BI


The project presents an interactive sales analytics dashboard developed using Power BI to analyse Adidas Sales performance across multiple business dimensions. They include time, geography, category of the product and retailers. The dashboard helps stakeholders understand the KPIs &amp; identify opportunities for improving revenue and profitability.

## Business Problem

Adidas operates in a highly competitive global sportswear market. For it to remain competitive, company must continuously monitor sales performance and understand the drivers behind the profitability and revenue. 

This Power BI project addresses the central analytics that enable decision makers to: 

1. Track Overall sales performance.

2. Identify the regions that are highly and lowly performing.

3. Evaluate the deman of products

4. Monitor the profitability metrics.

## Business Requirements

The Power BI analysis aims to deliver insights that support:

1. Improved understanding of sales dynamics and performance drivers

2. Identification of geographical areas with high and low sales potential

3. Insights into product performance for inventory and marketing decisions

4. Evaluation of pricing strategies

5. Identification of opportunities to optimize profit and sales performance

## KPI's

#### Total Sales

Measures the overall revenue generated from Adidas product sales.

#### Operating Profit

Shows the total profit generated after operating expenses.

#### Total Units Sold

Tracks the volume of products sold, providing insight into product demand.

#### Price per Unit

Calculates the average selling price per product unit, helping assess pricing strategy.

#### Operating Margin

Indicates the profitability percentage, showing how efficiently sales generate profit.

## Dashboard Visualizations

#### 1. Total Sales by Month

Displays monthly sales trends to identify:

Peak sales periods

Seasonal patterns

Periods where the performance is declining. 

This helps businesses plan marketing campaigns and inventory levels.

#### 2. Total Sales by State - The Map

A geographical map showing sales distribution across states.

This helps identify:

High-performing regions.

Untapped markets

Regions requiring strategic attention

#### 3. Total Sales by Region - Donut Chart

Illustrates the percentage contribution of each region to total sales.

This visualization supports regional sales strategy planning.

#### 4. Total Sales by Product - Bar Chart

Shows how different product categories contribute to total revenue.

Examples include:

Men's Street Footwear

Women's Athletic Footwear

Through this, businesses understand which product lines drive the most revenue.

#### 5. Total Sales by Retailer - Bar Chart

Displays sales contributions from different retail partners such as: 

West Gear

Foot Locker

Sports Direct

Kohl's

Amazon

Walmart

This analysis helps identify key retail partners and distribution channels.

## Key Insights

Some insights from the dashboard include:

Adidas generated approximately $900M in total sales during the analysis period.

Operating profit reached $332M, indicating strong profitability.

Sales peak during mid-year and end-of-year periods, Shows seasonal buying patterns.

Certain retailers contribute significantly more revenue, highlighting strong partnerships.

Product categories like Men's Street Footwear generate the highest revenue.
